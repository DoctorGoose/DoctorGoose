About the Founder

Dr. Jordan J.A. Weaver

Founder & Lead Researcher


Driven by a passion for unraveling the mysteries of the immune system and leveraging data analysis and machine learning, Dr. Jordan J.A. Weaver is a highly accomplished chemical engineer specializing in data analysis, mechanistic modeling, and machine learning for immunity-related research. With a Doctor of Philosophy in Chemical Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh.


As a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Tennessee Health Science Center, Jordan has delved into the intricate world of viral infections, specifically focusing on modeling the innate immune response to influenza and SARS-CoV-2. Their expertise in developing predictive models and utilizing information theory has provided invaluable insights into the dynamics of immune response and viral replication.

Expanding the Horizons of Life and Intelligence


At Stellar Perspectives, we are inspired by the vast potential of life and intelligence within the universe.


Our Vision


  • Understanding Our Place: Recognizing that complex life, as we know it, is a unique and emergent phenomenon, we advocate for expanding our sphere of influence within the universe. This expansion is not just about reaching out into the cosmos but also about deepening our understanding and stewardship of our own planet.

  • Encountering Intelligence: As we extend our reach, we open possibilities for encountering other forms of intelligence. Such interactions could lead to unprecedented advancements in knowledge, technology, and a deeper understanding of life.

  • Embracing Our Potential: We believe in the power of self-replicating, intelligent systems - a concept not limited to machines but inherent in ourselves. By exploring and embracing this potential, we aim to influence our future both on Earth and in the wider universe.

  • Navigating Challenges: Our journey requires us to navigate the challenges and opportunities of our time. From environmental stewardship to technological innovation, we are committed to guiding humanity towards a sustainable and prosperous future.

Our Approach


Through cutting-edge research in machine learning, biology, and sustainability, we strive to unlock new horizons for intelligent life. We believe in the power of collaboration - bringing together diverse minds and perspectives to tackle the complex challenges of our time. In our pursuit to expand life's influence, we remain committed to ethical considerations, ensuring that our actions benefit not just humanity but all life forms.


Join Us: We invite you to join us on this extraordinary journey, as we work towards a future where life and intelligence flourish, both on our home planet and beyond.
