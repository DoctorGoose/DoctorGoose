Welcome to the Isle of Woad

Nestled in the embrace of the vast sea, the Isle of Woad is an enigmatic land brimming with ancient magic, perilous landscapes, and hidden secrets. An isle where the whispers of the past intertwine with the rustle of the present, adventurers and scholars alike are drawn to its shores in search of glory, knowledge, and the mystical Red Woad.

Geography

The island is a mosaic of diverse terrains, each with its own character and lore. To the north, the imposing Mount Korrigan pierces the sky, its peaks shrouded in the mysteries of the ancients. To the south, the tranquil Naturewood and Starfell Dunes paint a serene contrast, with the azure waves of the sea lapping at the isle's sandy borders.
