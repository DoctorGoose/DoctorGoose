Room 1: Gnome's Last Stand

The air is thick with the scent of mold and the faint traces of old parchment. The walls are lined with decayed bookshelves. The gnome's bones are scattered, a sad reminder of the flood's might. An encounter with the gnome's spirit awaits, offering hints and warnings.

Room 2: The Pedestal's Key

The pedestal is carved with intricate runes that glow with arcane energy. Dusty tomes lie open, their pages whispering ancient secrets. But beware, spectral hands threaten to drag the unwary into the underworld.

Room 3: The Chest of Promises

This chamber, eerily tidy, holds a chest that seems to invite the curious. Opening it releases a ghostly librarian who offers a riddle crucial to the journey ahead.

Room 4: The Golden Reward

Golden light spills from the chest, illuminating murals of the basilica's prime. But is the gold as it seems, or is there danger lurking within?

Room 5: The Three-Keyed Gate

The mouth-shaped gate demands a sequence of keys, with the wrong choice yielding a noxious retribution. Clues from the archive guide the correct pattern.

Room 6: Under the Rubble

Amidst the broken columns and torn tapestries, elemental guardians protect hidden treasures. Disturb the rubble, and awaken the earth's wrath.
