Coldhall

Neighborhoods and Features

  • Castleward: The fortified governmental heart of Coldhall.
  • Lightwell: Known for its bright, never-extinguished lanterns and artisan community.
  • Honeywalk: Lined with confectioners and famous for its mead and beekeepers.
  • Beerpole: The bustling center of breweries and taverns.
  • Cold Square: The commercial hub with a diverse mix of cultures and goods.
  • The Rock: The defensively strong district, possibly housing the navy.
  • The Docks: The vital bustling heart of the city’s trade.

Expanding on Coldhall

Trade: Coldhall thrives on trade, with powerful guilds and a secretive black market.

Sea: The waters near Coldhall are ripe with legends and potential underwater quests.

Adventure Hook: The blockade led by a pirate captain searching for a precious item within the city.

Exploring the relationships between guilds, government, pirates, and sea creatures presents many narrative possibilities. The blockade’s economic impact creates opportunities for smuggling and civil unrest, while naval expeditions could introduce new allies or enemies from the deep.
