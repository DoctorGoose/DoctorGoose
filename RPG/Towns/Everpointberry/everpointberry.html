The Enclave of Mystic Horticulture

Everpointberry is an enclave of mystic horticulture and alchemical mastery, where nature's encroachment could be both a hazard and a mystery to unravel.

Adventure awaits: The town is on the edge of a mysterious forest, with unique flora and fauna that serve as the backbone for its thriving economy. As the annual Berry Harvest Festival approaches, a dark force from within the forest seeks to reclaim the land. Heroes are needed to investigate and push back the encroachment, ensuring that Everpointberry remains a haven for alchemists and scholars alike.

Explore the Pig Walk where magical boars reveal truffles of power, defend the Mistgate against the creeping forest, and delve into the Heatherside where potent berries grow. Every district, every berry, every whisper of the trees tells a story in Everpointberry.
