Deephill

The Quarters

  • The Lodge: A manor or town hall that signifies authority.
  • Old Ward: The historic heart of Deephill, rich with tradition.
  • Docks Quarter: The bustling economic center facing undead threats.
  • Long Garden: The agricultural core, vital to the village's survival.
  • Fey Walk: A mystical path, possibly a bridge between realms.

Agriculture

Deephill's prosperity from farming is threatened by mysterious thefts, which could be linked to supernatural or mundane forces.

Danger

The proximity to Bloodmarsh brings a constant threat of undead, requiring vigilance and defense from the villagers.

Adventure Hook

The thefts might be tied to a dark power in Bloodmarsh, leading the adventurers on a quest to uncover and confront this evil.

Intertwining these elements creates a rich tapestry of lore and intrigue for Deephill, as adventurers navigate a complex web of village politics, ancient secrets, and the encroaching dread from Bloodmarsh.
