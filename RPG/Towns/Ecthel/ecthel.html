Ecthel

Neighborhoods and Features

  • Seven Gardens: A series of enchanted gardens, each with unique properties.
  • Dockleaf District: The main harbor area for trade and travel.
  • Winegate: The social hub, known for vineyards and taverns.
  • Dogate: A bustling center of commerce.
  • Castle District: The political center and the elders' seat of power.
  • Light Borough: The spiritual heart, with temples and centers of learning.
  • Cabbage Ridge: The agricultural area, known for its cabbage farms.

Expanding on Ecthel

Government and Society: Governed by a council of elders with traditions rooted in arcane power.

Inhabitants: Elven druids and historians dedicated to lore and the natural order.

Culture and Magic: A society where magic is a part of everyday life and celebrations.

Adventure and Conflict: The city faces a threat from a reawakened entity within the Emberwood.

Mysteries and Secrets: Each district has its own secrets and mysteries to uncover.

Economy and Trade: Trade with the outside world is essential for obtaining rare goods.

Defense and Military: The elite guards, known as the Shadowleaves, are trained in combat and arcane arts.
