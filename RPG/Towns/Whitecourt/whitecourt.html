Whitecourt

A bastion of chivalry and craftsmanship

The Town of Whitecourt

Whitecourt is a small community of just 35 inhabitants, each contributing to the town's survival. Renowned for its skilled stonemasons and the unique marble found in the mountains, Whitecourt stands as a testament to resilience and craftsmanship.

The knightly order of Whitecourt, guardians of the mountain passes, are ever vigilant against the threat of the ancient dragon rumored to have awakened. The knights are the protectors of the town, its people, and the secrets of their stonemasonry craft.

Adventurers come to Whitecourt drawn by tales of the dragon and the promise of riches. The town is a focal point for stories of protection, ancient secrets, and the battle against a legendary beast.
